World University Ranking Analysis

The United States is still the country with the strongest higher education. Eight of the top 10 universities in the world belong to the United States and the other two are the United Kingdom. The following is a specific data visualization analysis.

The Harvard university was 4 years in a row in the first place, Its fractional line maintains at 100 all the time. Yale university entered the rankings in 2012-2014, but not in 2015. There are also some universities that come from behind, such as the University of Chicago and Cornell University, The University of Chicago did not enter the rankings in 2012, but it has been in the rankings from 2013 to 2015. And Cornell University was ranked only in 2015.
